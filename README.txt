Valetudo for Homey brings your Valetudo-powered robot vacuum into the Homey ecosystem. Control cleaning, monitor battery, and manage multi-floor setups directly from Homey. A built-in map widget gives you a live view of your robot at work.

Compatible with robot vacuums from Roborock, Dreame, Xiaomi, Viomi, Eureka, Cecotec, MOVA, and Proscenic. Only specific models within each brand are supported, and some features may vary depending on your model. Check the Valetudo documentation for a full list of supported robots before installing. Whether you have one floor or several, Valetudo for Homey keeps everything in sync so you can automate your cleaning from a single place.
